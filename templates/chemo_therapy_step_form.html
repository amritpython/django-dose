As you are (or due to be) receiving chemotherapy, unfortunately our DOSE product is contraindicated. Using DOSE whilst receiving chemotherapy can increase your risks of skin sensitivity and potentially worsen chemotherapy related hair loss. You may wish to visit our DOSE DIRECTORY for professionals who can help to support you in managing your hair loss.

Next

As DOSE POST-CHEMO contains Minoxidil, unfortunately you will not be suitable to use this medication. You may wish to discuss alternatives with our recommended DOSE DIRECTORY dermatologists or consider our recommended non-pharmaceutical brands.

Next

Any scalp inflammation or injury may increase your risk of developing irritation with DOSE products. Please arrange to see your GP or one of our recommended DOSE DIRECTORY dermatologists to diagnose and treat your scalp condition before purchasing DOSE for your hair.
